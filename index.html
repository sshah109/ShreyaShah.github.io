AirBnB Insights for Seattle Hosts (2016 Analysis)

Navigating the competitive landscape of the AirBnB market can be daunting for hosts, especially in bustling metropolitan areas. This dashboard is designed to empower a Seattle-based AirBnB host to strategically grow their business using data from 2016. The interactive visuals enable the host to identify prime investment areas and optimal property types by analyzing the number of bedrooms. By examining the distribution of properties, the host can gauge the competition and make informed decisions. Ultimately, this dashboard serves as a comprehensive research tool, providing critical insights for a Seattle AirBnB host aiming to expand their business effectively.
